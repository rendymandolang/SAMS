# SHRiS — Super Human Resource Information System

SHRiS is the workforce module of SuperSoft Enterprise. Its first production foundation is company-scoped and ready to synchronize with the other subscribed modules.

## Available foundation

- Position and organization assignment by branch and department.
- Employee onboarding and lifecycle status, including contract and termination controls.
- Optional linkage between an employee record and a SuperSoft user account.
- Configurable leave types, annual balances, workday calculation, approval, rejection, and cancellation.
- Sensitive data separated by permission.
- Employee PDF/image documents encrypted before storage, integrity checked on download, and compatible with company-owned cloud storage.
- Audit logs for employee lifecycle, leave decisions, and protected-document access.
- SHRiS records included in encrypted company backups.

## Next SHRiS cores

1. Attendance, work schedules, shifts, public holidays, overtime, and device/API imports.
2. Payroll configuration, recurring components, tax/BPJS rules, payroll runs, payslips, and posting into SaS.
3. Recruitment, onboarding checklist, probation review, performance, training, and disciplinary records.
4. Employee and manager self-service with delegated approvals and notifications.

Payroll will post controlled journals into SaS instead of maintaining a separate financial ledger. Attendance and payroll will retain source evidence and approval history so every posted amount can be traced.
